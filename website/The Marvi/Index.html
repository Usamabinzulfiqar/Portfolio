In today's rapidly evolving world, technology has become an integral part of our daily lives. Form the way we communicate to the way we gather information, technology has revolutionized every aspect of our existence. One of the most significant developments in recent times is the advent of information technology.

Information technology, often abbreviated as IT, refers to the use, development, and management of computer system, software, networks and electronic devices to store, process , transmit, and retrieve digital information.It encompasses a wide range of technologies, including but not limited to computers,mobile devices,the internet,tele systems, software applications, databases,and much more.
